Genshin Impact Character Quiz
📜 How to Play
1
You will be shown an accessory or item belonging to a playable Genshin Impact character.
2
You have 3 tries to guess the correct character name.
3
Your first wrong answer will reveal a hint to help you out.
The hint is not available before your first guess.
4
If you use all 3 tries without guessing correctly, the answer will be revealed.
5
Score 5 out of 5 to earn a special reward — good luck, Traveler! ✨
